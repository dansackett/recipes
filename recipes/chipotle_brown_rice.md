# Chipotle Brown Rice

Excellent cilantro lime rice made in the instant pot.

## Timing

| Prep Time | Cook Time  |
| --------- | ---------- |
| 1 Minute  | 22 Minutes |

## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown rice rinsed
- 2 1/2 cups vegetable broth
- 1/2 tsp. sea salt
- 2 Tbsp. lime juice
- 1 tsp. olive oil
- 1 tsp. garlic minced
- 1/4 cup cilantro leaves only, finely chopped

## Utilities

- Instant Pot
