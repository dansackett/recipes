# Taco Seasoning

Simple taco seasoning using the core spices.

## Timing

| Prep Time  | Cook Time  |
| ---------- | ---------- |
| 5 Minutes | - |

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp chilli powder
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper
- 1/4 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp sea salt
- 1 tsp ground black pepper
